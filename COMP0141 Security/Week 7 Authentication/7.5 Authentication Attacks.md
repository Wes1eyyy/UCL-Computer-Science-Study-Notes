## Week 7 – Authentication Attacks 🛡️

### 🔑 Phishing (钓鱼攻击)

- Phishing → via email (邮件)
    
- Vishing → via phone, voice phishing (电话钓鱼，可用 deepfake)
    
- Smishing → via SMS (短信钓鱼)
    
- Pharming → redirect or fake websites to enhance phishing (伪装网站增强钓鱼)
    

---

### 🌐 Phishing Website & Typosquatting (钓鱼网站与拼写劫持)

- Phishing websites fake login pages (伪造登录页)
    
- Typosquatting → register misspelled domains (拼写劫持域名)
    
- Can include:
    
    - fake HTTPS (伪造锁图标)
        
    - confusing domain names (`trustedbank.com@evilsite.com`)
        

---

### 🎯 Other Attacks on ‘What You Know’ (攻击你知道的东西)

- Capture attacks (捕获攻击):
    
    - Skimming (侧录器, e.g. ATM)
        
    - Keylogging (键盘记录器)
        
    - Packet sniffing (抓包监听, 未加密流量)
        
- Intimate partner violence (亲密伴侣暴力, 不用猜密码)
    
- Observation attacks (肩膀偷窥, shoulder-surfing)
    
- Side-channel attacks (侧信道, 如按键声/油迹)
    
- Coercion (橡皮管攻击, 暴力胁迫)
    

---

### 🔒 Attacks on ‘What You Have’ (攻击你拥有的东西)

- Replay attacks (重放攻击):  
    → reuse captured one-time passwords (重复使用一次性密码)  
    → preventable by session IDs (用会话ID防止)
    

---

### 🚪 Access Control (访问控制)

- Authentication ≠ Authorization (认证 ≠ 授权)
    
- After login, system still checks access rights (登录后仍需检查访问权限)
